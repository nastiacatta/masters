# Declaration of generative-AI use

Per the Dyson School of Design Engineering AI policy, this notice
records the generative-AI tools used during the preparation of this
report and the purpose for which they were used.

**Tools.** Anthropic Claude and GitHub Copilot.

**Where used.**
- Drafting, editing, and proofreading prose in this report and the
  accompanying presentation materials.
- Scaffolding code for experiments, plotting, and the companion
  dashboard.
- Cross-checking derivations and literature against the primary
  sources cited in Chapter~\ref{ch:intro}.

**Where not used.**
- No AI tool generated experimental results, numerical outputs, or
  figures. Every number in this report is produced by the codebase
  described in Chapter~\ref{ch:methodology} and
  Appendix~\ref{app:training-details}.

**Attribution.** Tool outputs were treated as third-party
suggestions. No output is used verbatim without review,
verification against primary sources, and editing into the
author's own voice. All ideas, judgements, and final prose are the
author's responsibility.
