# Results {#ch:results}

Results follow in four sections. Section~\ref{ch:synthetic} reports
synthetic experiments that verify mechanism correctness and recover
the true skill ordering on controlled data. Section~\ref{ch:real}
reports real-data results on Elia offshore wind and
electricity-imbalance prices, with an external comparison against
Elia's own operational forecast, the conditional reading of the
headline CRPS gain, and a threats-to-validity audit.
Section~\ref{ch:recalibration} develops a rolling isotonic
recalibration layer as a post-hoc remedy for the
\citet{ranjan2010combining} linear-pool miscalibration.
Section~\ref{ch:robustness} evaluates the mechanism against a
theory-grounded adversary catalogue and interprets each attack in
place. Each section opens with the question it addresses;
interpretation sits next to the numbers rather than in a separate
discussion chapter.
