# Results {#ch:results}

The results are organised into four sections. Section~\ref{ch:synthetic}
reports the synthetic experiments that establish mechanism
correctness and recover the true skill ordering on controlled data.
Section~\ref{ch:real} reports real-data results on Elia offshore
wind and electricity-imbalance prices, together with an external
comparison against Elia's own operational forecast, the conditional
interpretation of the headline CRPS gain, and a threats-to-validity
audit. Section~\ref{ch:recalibration} develops the rolling isotonic
recalibration layer as a post-hoc remedy for the
\citet{ranjan2010combining} linear-pool miscalibration.
Section~\ref{ch:robustness} evaluates the mechanism against the
theory-grounded adversary catalogue and interprets each attack's
result in place. Each section opens with a short statement of the
question it addresses; interpretation sits next to the numbers
rather than in a separate discussion chapter.
